DeltaX.ai, Seoul — 2023–2025

Led development and deployment of production-grade in-cabin perception systems using IR cameras and embedded AI hardware.

In-Cabin Monitoring (ICMS):

  • Seatbelt detection, driver hands-on/off detection, drowsiness detection, child presence detection, occupancy monitoring, and driver behaviour analysis using IR camera data
  • Deployed models on NVIDIA Jetson, TI boards, and Hailo platforms using quantization and knowledge distillation

ADAS:

  • Real-time multi-class semantic segmentation for road scene understanding (pothole, curb, parking, speed bump, crack) optimized for TI edge boards
  • Lane Departure Warning (LDW) and Forward Collision Warning (FCW) via monocular depth estimation

Tools & Sensors: PyTorch · IR/Thermal cameras · ONNX · TensorRT · TI board · NVIDIA Jetson · Hailo
