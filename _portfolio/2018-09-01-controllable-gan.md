**Soongsil University System Software Lab — 2018–2023**

PhD dissertation research on controllable unsupervised generative adversarial networks.

**Highlights:**

- Designed a controllable unsupervised GAN architecture that disentangles content and style representations without label supervision
- Applied mixing regularization to enforce content consistency across multiple domains, enabling truly unsupervised domain translation
- Developed a projection encoder for structured latent space learning, supporting controllable attribute manipulation
- Applications include: image style transfer, facial attribute editing, multi-domain translation, and character image synthesis

**Publications:**

- Hassan, A. U., Lee, H., & Choi, J. *Exploiting mixing regularization for truly unsupervised font synthesis.* Pattern Recognition Letters, 169, 35–42. (2023)
- Hassan, A. U., Memon, I., & Choi, J. *Learning font-style space using style-guided discriminator for few-shot font generation.* Expert Systems With Applications, 242, 122817. (2024)

**Dissertation:** *Unsupervised Image Generation for Multiple Domains based on Mixing Regularization and Projection Encoder* (Soongsil University, 2023)

**Tools:** PyTorch · GANs · Contrastive Learning · Metric Learning
